# Atelier ROI Visio

A premium discovery-call interface for consultants, freelancers, and automation specialists who want to turn live conversations into clear business decisions.

Atelier ROI Visio helps structure a client call around three essential outcomes:

- clarify the business need,
- shape a first solution direction,
- estimate a practical ROI,
- generate reusable outputs for delivery or follow-up.

Built for screen-sharing, the tool is designed to feel clean on call, useful in operations, and credible as a client-facing deliverable.

## Why it exists

Discovery calls often end with scattered notes, vague next steps, and too much uncertainty.

Atelier ROI Visio was created to make that moment more concrete. Instead of leaving the call with only impressions, the consultant leaves with a structured session, a clearer opportunity, and ready-to-use outputs.

## What it does

Atelier ROI Visio combines:

- a guided multi-step discovery flow,
- consultant mode and client mode,
- local autosave,
- grouped export,
- reusable extraction files,
- and a premium printable client report.

The result is a tool that sits between consulting, automation discovery, and pre-sales enablement.

## Core benefits

### Better calls

Keep the discussion focused on business problems, not technical noise.

### Better handoff

Turn the session into structured artifacts that can feed delivery, automation design, or AI-assisted drafting.

### Better positioning

Present the work as a premium advisory process, not just a shared form.
